    An online Focous Timer to boost your productivity

    What is Moocuss ???

    Moocuss is a customizable focous timer that works on desktop & mobile browser. The aim of this app is to help you focus on any task you are working on, such as study, writing, or coding. This app is inspired by Pomodoro Technique which is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo.

    What is Pomodoro Technique?

    The Pomodoro Technique, developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s, is a time management method that enhances productivity through focused intervals. Named after the Italian word for "tomato," it originated from Cirillo's use of a tomato-shaped kitchen timer. The technique involves breaking work into 25-minute intervals, called "Pomodoros," followed by a short break. After four cycles, take a longer break. The structured approach helps maintain concentration, manage distractions, and prevent burnout. Its simplicity and adaptability have made it popular worldwide, aiding individuals in boosting efficiency and completing tasks with increased concentration and reduced procrastination.

    How to use the Pomodoro Technique?

    Set a Clear Goal:
    Identify a specific task and define it clearly before starting the Pomodoro session.
    Set the Timer:
    Use a timer, preferably set to 25 minutes (one Pomodoro), to break down your work into focused intervals.
    Work on the Task:
    During the Pomodoro session, concentrate solely on the chosen task, minimizing distractions and interruptions.
    Take a Short Break:
    After completing a Pomodoro, take a 5-minute break to relax and recharge before starting the next focused interval.
    Repeat and Take a Longer Break:
    After four Pomodoros, take a longer break (15-30 minutes) to rest and rejuvenate before beginning a new cycle if necessary.
